To summarise, at the beginning of each trial, you are to position your orange bucket to where you think you will be able to catch the flying coin with the left/right ARROW keys.

Press the SPACEBAR to confirm the bucket position.

You will then indicate how confident you are that the coin will land in the orange bucket on the confidence meter with the left/right ARROW keys. Please do your best to rate your confidence accurately and do take advantage of the whole rating scale.

After you have chosen your confidence level in how likely you believe the coin is going to land in your orange bucket, press SPACEBAR again to let the coin fly.

Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.

On the top right corner of the screen, there will be a counter to show you how many points you've collected along the way.

First, you will do one practice block of 10 coins to get used to the task.
After, you will have to complete a short quiz relating to task.

You will need to answer all of the quiz questions correctly to move on to the experiment - otherwise we will bring you through the instructions and practice again.


When you're ready to start the practice, click Next.
